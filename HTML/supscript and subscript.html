SUPERSCRIPT AND SUBSCRIPT

SUPERSCRIPT

The <sup> element is used to contain characters that contains superscript such as the suffixes of dates or mathematical concepts of raising a number power

SUBSCRIPT

The <sub> element is used for characters that contains subscript such as foot notes or chemical formulas.
